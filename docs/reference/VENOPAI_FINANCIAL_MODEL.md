2. Cost Structure Overview

Venopai costs are divided into:

1. Fixed Costs


2. Variable Costs

---

3. Fixed Costs (Monthly)

Examples:

Domain & Hosting (Vercel + Backend Hosting)

Cloudinary storage (if paid tier)

Internet & electricity

Equipment depreciation (printer, heat press)


Example Estimate (Early Stage):

Hosting: ₹1500 Cloud storage: ₹800 Electricity & utilities: ₹2000 Misc tools: ₹1000

Estimated Fixed Monthly Cost: ₹5,000 – ₹8,000


---

4. Variable Cost Per T-Shirt (Example Calculation)

Blank T-shirt cost: ₹180 Printing ink/material: ₹40 Packaging: ₹20 Label/branding: ₹10

Total Base Cost: ₹250

Courier charge (average): ₹60

Total Production Cost Per Unit: ₹310


---

5. Razorpay Transaction Charges

Typical online payment charge: ~2% + GST per transaction

If selling price = ₹499 Razorpay fee ≈ ₹10–₹15

Effective Revenue After Payment Fee: ₹499 – ₹15 = ₹484


---

6. Profit Calculation Example

Selling Price: ₹499 Total Cost (Production + Courier): ₹310 Payment Fee: ₹15

Total Expense: ₹325

Net Profit Per T-Shirt: ₹499 – ₹325 = ₹174

Profit Margin ≈ 34%

7. Custom Printing Pricing Strategy

Formula:

Base Product Cost

Printing Complexity Cost

Platform Margin (30–40%)

Payment Fee Buffer


Example:

Base shirt: ₹180 Heavy design print: ₹80 Packaging: ₹20 Courier: ₹60

Total Cost: ₹340

Add 35% margin → ₹460–₹499 final price


---

8. Student Project Manufacturing Pricing

Cost Components:

Raw materials

Electronics components (if applicable)

Labor time

Testing time

Packaging

Courier


Recommended Strategy: Minimum 40% margin (because of higher effort)

Example: Material Cost: ₹1,200 Labor Value: ₹500 Packaging & Courier: ₹200

Total Cost: ₹1,900

Selling Price (40% margin): ₹2,660

Profit ≈ ₹760


---

9. Break-Even Analysis

Formula:

Break-Even Units = Fixed Monthly Cost / Profit Per Unit

Example:

Fixed Cost: ₹6,000 Profit per T-shirt: ₹174

Break-Even Units: 6000 / 174 ≈ 35 T-shirts per month

Meaning: After 35 sales, you start making net profit.


---

10. Monthly Revenue Projection (Example)

If 100 T-shirts sold/month:

Revenue: 100 × ₹499 = ₹49,900 Profit per unit: ₹174 Total Gross Profit: ₹17,400 Minus Fixed Cost (~₹6,000)

Net Monthly Profit ≈ ₹11,400
